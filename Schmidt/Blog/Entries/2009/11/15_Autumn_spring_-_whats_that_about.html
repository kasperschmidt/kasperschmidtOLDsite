Autumn spring - what’s that about?

 

Hi guys...

Back again from a short visit to DK. Didn’t really do much - worked a bit and just spend time with Mette. Of course I also dropped by my parents place for lunch - I really miss proper rugbrød and danish toppings like leverpostej, frikadeller, rullepølse and smoked mackerel  - thanks mom :) As you might guess I’m hungry... the potatoes are in the owen and the smell doesn’t really help on my hunger - but then I thought that I could blog a bit while the dinner is finishing. So here I am, blogging about nothing and everything. But as mentioned I was in Denmark for a short visit last weekend. Besides Mette and my parents I also found the time to drop by my brother before I flew back monday morning. My brother, his girlfriend (which I met for the first time - nice girl...) just chilled to the tv, ordered some sushi and had a couple of games of Klickado which is actually a small funny ‘inverse mikado’ game - especially because you suck at it Martin and Annkatrine :D Victory is sweet :p

Since I got back to Germany monday I have been working on Hα velocity mapping of NGC7331 to get started on another part of my PhD project.

Yesterday I went out with M&M to celebrate Dr. Rodon which was a pleasant way to end a dark and rainy week in Heidelberg... speaking about the whether - they said in the news the other day that tuesday it will be around 19 degrees in Baden-Württemberg... wtf is that about? its mid november and the all-mighty gives us spring temperatures - well no need to complain - me like!

BibBibBib said the oven, meaning my dinner is (almost) ready. Talk (or write) to you soon.


Ciao Bella
