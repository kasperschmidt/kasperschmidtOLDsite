It started so nice and then it ended so bad... well maybe not bad, but at least they could have advanced to the quarters. Besides watching football I have started reading up for the defense - 2 weeks left and counting. I must admit it has actually been good to brush up all the basic astrophysics stuff; it’s like much of it makes much more sense now that I have been doing this for 4 years without worrying about the next exam and just focused on the research. But I must also admit that it’s not all of the things I brush up which are equally interesting - no offense but sunspots, asteroid chemical composition, and planet formation is not really my thing... But the preparation is moving forward which is good.


Friday I went out for some beers to focus on something else (or more correctly focus on nothing really) with a couple of the guys from basket. We ended up going all in which was really what I needed. Wasn’t that productive yesterday but I’m pretty sure that the stress release that alcohol after all is will help me get through the last two weeks. However, waking up with a desert-mouth and then seeing that one of my mates from University posted the song below on FB made yesterday tough. I miss you Faxe Kondi!


Besides I don’t have much to report. I’ll keep you updated
