Euro 2012 - It’s on!

 

Unbelievable... amazing!

 

Holland 0 - Denmark 1 !!! Hell YEAH!!!


Holy crap that was a crazy match! It was definitely the longest second half ever. Good that I had my mental trips to Isle of Jura to calm my nerves... I don’t know if it was ‘deserved’ but if they can’t score it is pretty hard to win! The tune in the YouTube clip below was played immediately after the win on the danish national radio. The text is something like:


“Holland is on hash, Holland is on hash. Now it’s 1-0. Orange bong-heads it was an own goal. Holland is on hash. What’s up Holland you want a tackle? You’re half bald guys on the pitch... we’re not afraid of Van Persie and Robben their bodies are filled with skunk and hash and we’re on top. In Scotland they have a lot of cool clans. In Holland they only have crappy Tulips... Holland is on Hash... it’s 1-0“ (freely translated).


A good laugh, but also pretty cocky ;) But what the heck. We won against Holland! A victory against Holland pretty much makes the Euro 2012 a success! Getting through from the group stage would be oretty awesome, though.


1 down 2 to go - we are ready for you Portugal and Germany!


I guess this makes me ready for tomorrow when the last stretch for the PhD defense sets in with reading, reading and more reading.
