A new Life in Heidelberg

 

Since the last entry in my blog, i have moved to Heidelberg in southern Germany to start a new life as PhD student at MPIA in Heidelberg. Actually so far the picture above (my office) describes all I have experienced so far. There has been A LOT of paperwork, bank, institute, insurance, etc., and I’m afraid there is plenty left. Furthermore I have been moving around since for the first month or so I only have temporary places to live. On the left are some pictures from an apartment I lived in the first week, and on right the apartment I’m living in at the time (see also the picture section). Luckily I have found a place to live permanently from the first of november, so one month and I’m settled for good here. It’s a  small flat on 30 m^2 with a small kitchen and a bathroom located in the southern part of the city, a place called Rohrbach. So from November 1st I’m ready for visitors;)

Saturday I’m planing to go see a bit more of the city if the weather allows it, and tomorrow I’m wondering of taking a run alongside the Neckar river. Just to get out a bit, and not get stuck in the work already. That can wait until my last year and the approaching of the hand-in of my thesis ;)

Beside that there is not much to tell about my new life down here - but I’m sure that much will come!
